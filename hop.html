Hopdoddy Burger Bar

Our Hopdoddy team works hard to give you hospitality from the heart from the moment you step into our location. One of our favorite burgers is the Llano Poblano, made with Certified Piedmontese beef, steakhouse bacon, pepper jack cheese, seared Poblano peppers, and chipotle aioli. This burger is a crowd favorite - hot and mouthwatering and made fresh to order. If you’re looking for something with more of a kick, try the incredible El Diablo. It has Tillamook pepper jack cheese, habanero and serrano peppers, caramelized onions, salsa roja, and chipotle aioli. Just hold on to your hat because this burger packs a punch! What’s a burger without fries? Our Parmesan Truffle Fries are famous for a reason... these amazing, hand cut fries are double blanched, seasoned with truffle oil, parmesan, chives, and served with truffle aioli. Try these fries with your burger!
